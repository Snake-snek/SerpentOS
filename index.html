The Genesis of Serpent OS

Post #1 | 2026

Welcome to the very first post of the Serpent OS project. Let's be clear from the start: there is nothing to download here yet. This is the earliest stage of development—the "First Breath" of a new independent operating system.

Behind this project stands the Serpent Team. To be completely honest, right now, the "team" consists of just one person. This is why the development process might take a significant amount of time. I am building this alone, and the road ahead is long.

Currently, this website looks like a simple presentation (perhaps a bit "raw," like a first-grader's project), but it is a starting point. As the system evolves from a collection of build scripts into a functional Linux distribution, this site will grow with it. We are moving away from the legacy GCC toolchain in favor of a modern LLVM-based core.

Stay tuned. The serpent is beginning to stir.
